Outliers can be identified by using boxplots, boxplots(IQR) or using Z scores. The boxplot(IQR) method using the 1.5xIQR- Q1 (lower limit) and Q3+ 1.5xIQR (upper limit) to eliminate outliers The z-score value is the measure of how far the data point is from the mean = 0 and SD = 1, i.e., a normal deviation. We consider the z scores between -3 to 3 to eliminate outliers. You can also remove all outliers less than the 5th percentile and greater than the 95th percentile
